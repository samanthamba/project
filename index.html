How to Prevent Fires at Home

Attend to the Stove

Fire can only take seconds before breaking out so if you are cooking and need to step out a bit, either turn it off or have someone watch over it.

Flammable Materials

Make sure to keep any flammable materials stored properly to avoid any reactions that can start a fire.

Fire Extinguisher

It is best that at all times, a fire extinguisher will be present so that in any case that a fire may start, you will be to stop it in an instant. However, if a fire extinguisher is not present, there are other methods you can use to stop the fire through cloths, water with sope, and etc.

Maintainance of Wires

Always maintain your wires because any form of damaged wire can become a huge fire hazzard. Also keep in mind that when wires overheat, you must check the placement to avoid sudden outbursts.
